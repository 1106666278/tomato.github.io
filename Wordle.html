About the Viral Word Puzzle Game

Wordle is a popular web-based word puzzle game where players have six attempts to guess a five-letter word. After each guess, the game provides feedback in the form of colored tiles, indicating whether letters are correct and in the correct position, correct but in the wrong position, or not in the word at all.

How to Play Wordle: The Rules

Type in a five-letter word and press Enter. The tiles will change color:

  • Green: The letter is correct and in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.

Use the feedback from each guess to deduce the correct word within six tries. A new puzzle is available each day.
